What ink is on your shelf ?

Tired of that messy old shelf and all the guessing games ? Wink is the answer.

Wink is an ink management system used to organize, stores and creates formulas for screen printers of all kinds.

How we can help?

Click and store.

With just a click from your smart phone, Wink instantly recognizes the colour of swatch you have mixed and organizes it in your own library.

Never be unsure again.

Oh oh! Lost the formula? No worries, Wink calculates formulas for every pantone colour.

In the shop and on the go.

No need to cart around colour swatches, Wink is your entire ink library available at a glance, wherever you are.
